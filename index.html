Have you thought what to do, when sitting in the end of a subway trolley facing the wall, and there are unknown people, you don't want to talk with, sitting front of you. You don't know where to look at, because you already looked at your shoes. Don’t worry solution is coming. It's Al's New Wings.

Al's New Wings is a helicopter rescue mobile game, made in Helsinki Metropolia University of Applied Sciences. The game is perfectly suitable for subway. Namely, one level takes couple of minutes to pass, So you can play the game between the subway stations, and you're ready to leave the train when it's needed.

Al's New Wings is nonviolent. Gameplay focuses rescue missions instead of shooting zombies with a shotgun. In the game you rescue humans and animals in trouble, goods from floating in the sea and putting down fires in the forest in different rescue scenarios.

The game is under further development, and it will be available for iPhone and Android devices.
